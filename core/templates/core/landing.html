The Problem

  • Maintenance and repairs are slow or unpredictable.
  • Tenant trust and payment reliability are difficult to manage remotely.
  • Communication breaks down across distance and time zones.
  • Manual workflows create delays, stress, and lost revenue.

Solution

Automation plus human expertise in one workflow:

  • Maintenance ticket creation and contractor routing.
  • Rent reminders, dues tracking, and late-payment alerts.
  • Centralized communication hub for tenants, landlords, and contractors.
  • Verified contractor network with real-time updates and reporting.

How It Works

  1. Tenant reports an issue, and a maintenance ticket is created automatically.
  2. The ticket is routed to the right local contractor.
  3. Contractor proposes timing, completes work, and updates status.
  4. Tenant and landlord receive centralized progress updates.
  5. Rent tracking and late-dues alerts run continuously.
  6. Landlord receives monthly operational and financial reporting.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Make property ownership stress-free through automation and reliable human support.

Mission: Simplify workflows, reduce operational risk, and improve landlord/tenant satisfaction globally.

Business Model

  • SaaS subscriptions for landlords (basic to premium).
  • Transaction fees for rent collection and maintenance payments.
  • Premium services like tenant placement and reporting upgrades.

Competitive Advantage

  • Remote-first product built for owners managing from abroad.
  • Three-way ecosystem: tenant, landlord, contractor in one system.
  • End-to-end automation from issue reporting to financial tracking.
  • Verified contractor network for trust and execution quality.
  • Centralized communication and real-time reporting for full visibility.
