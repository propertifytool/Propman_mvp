About Propertify

Who We Are

Propertify is a remote-first property management platform designed for owners who need dependable operations without being physically present. We combine software workflows with local execution so landlords can manage confidently from anywhere.

The Problem We Solve

Remote landlords often face maintenance delays, communication breakdowns, trust issues, and manual processes that consume time and increase risk. These gaps create stress for owners and inconsistent service for tenants.

Our Approach

  • Workflow automation: structured tickets, status tracking, and reporting.
  • Verified local contractors: trusted execution for repairs and maintenance.
  • Human support: hands-on coordination when automation alone is not enough.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Stress-free property ownership anywhere in the world.

Mission: Simplify property management, reduce risk, and improve transparency.
